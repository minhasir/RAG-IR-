Question: what was the result of the treaty of paris in 1763

Reference Answer: The signing of the treaty formally ended the Seven Years ' War , known as the French and Indian War in the North American theatre , and marked the beginning of an era of British dominance outside Europe . Great Britain and France each returned much of the territory that they had captured during the war , but Great Britain gained much of France 's possessions in North America . Additionally , Great Britain agreed to protect Roman Catholicism in the New World . The treaty did not involve Prussia and Austria as they signed a separate agreement , the Treaty of Hubertusburg , five days later .
