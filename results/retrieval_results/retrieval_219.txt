Question: what does the star on the ghana flag mean

Reference Answer: The red represents the blood of those who died in the country 's struggle for independence from the Great Britain , the gold represents the mineral wealth of the country , the green symbolises the country 's rich forests and natural wealth , and the black star is the symbol of African emancipation . The black star was adopted from the flag of the Black Star Line , a shipping line incorporated by Marcus Garvey that operated from 1919 to 1922 . It is where the Ghana national football team draw their nickname , the Black Stars , from .
